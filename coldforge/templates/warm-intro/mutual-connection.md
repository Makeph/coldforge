---
id: intro_mutual
name: Warm Intro — Mutual Connection
category: warm-intro
persona: Anyone reaching out where a shared contact has agreed to be named
use_case: The highest-converting cold email there is — a real mutual who said yes to being mentioned. Use their name early and honestly.
deliverability_notes: |
  Only name the mutual if they actually agreed. Put the name in the first line —
  it's the whole reason this gets opened and replied to. Keep the ask small and
  specific.
subject: "{{mutual}} suggested I reach out"
variables: [first_name, mutual, reason, my_context, ask, sender_name]
---

Hi {{first_name}},

{{mutual}} mentioned you when {{reason}} came up — said you'd be the right person to talk to.

Quick context: {{my_context}}. I'm not trying to sell you anything here; {{ask}}.

Would a short call work, or is there a better way to get 15 minutes of your read on it?

Thanks — and happy to loop {{mutual}} in if useful.

— {{sender_name}}
