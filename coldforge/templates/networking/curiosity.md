---
id: network_curiosity
name: Networking — No-Ask Curiosity
category: networking
persona: Someone building their network / exploring a role change, with no ask yet
use_case: First touch to a person whose work you genuinely admire, when you want a relationship, not a favour. The absence of an ask is the point — it stands out.
deliverability_notes: |
  Reference one specific thing they made, not their whole career. Do NOT ask for a
  job, a referral, or "to pick your brain". Keep it short enough that replying costs
  them nothing. One genuine, answerable question.
subject: "{{specific_thing}} — one question"
variables: [first_name, specific_thing, what_resonated, my_context, one_question, sender_name]
---

Hi {{first_name}},

{{specific_thing}} stuck with me — {{what_resonated}}.

I'm {{my_context}}, so I keep coming back to one thing I'd love your take on: {{one_question}}

No agenda beyond that — a one-line answer would genuinely make my week. And if it's easier to say in 15 minutes sometime, I'd take that too.

Either way, thanks for making the thing that prompted this.

— {{sender_name}}
