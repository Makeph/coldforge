---
id: sales_pain_point
name: Sales — Pain-Point Opener
category: sales
persona: Founder or AE selling a product that removes a specific, nameable pain
use_case: First touch when you can point at a concrete symptom the prospect is almost certainly feeling. Earns the reply by being recognisable, not clever.
deliverability_notes: |
  Open on the symptom, not your product. No "I hope this finds you well", no
  attachments, no calendar link in send #1. Keep it under 120 words and plaintext —
  HTML and images push first-touch cold mail toward Promotions/spam.
subject: "{{company}} + {{pain}}?"
variables: [first_name, company, pain, observation, outcome, sender_name]
---

Hi {{first_name}},

{{observation}} — which usually means {{pain}} is quietly eating time that should go elsewhere.

We help teams like {{company}} get rid of that part specifically: {{outcome}}. No rip-and-replace, no quarter-long rollout.

Worth a quick look? If it's not a priority right now, a one-line "not now" is a completely fine reply and I'll stop.

— {{sender_name}}
